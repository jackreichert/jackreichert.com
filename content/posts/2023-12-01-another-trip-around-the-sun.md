Last year I started a project to list [42 things I have learned](/2022/12/01/day-0-on-being-42-and-the-answer-to-the-ultimate-question-of-life-the-universe-and-everything/) until now. I had hoped to get through the list. In reality I got through a little more than 1/4 of my list.

Things happened, and I didn’t feel that my list would be appropriate to continue under those circumstances. [I was laid off](/2023/05/04/today-i-laid-off-with-20-of-shopify/), [I started a new job](/2023/07/25/how-i-did-it/), war broke out in my [homeland](/2022/04/11/star-of-david/).

I also saw that I liked taking my time musing over the idea before writing.

This year I hope to continue my list, maybe finish it. But as with many things, I believe that quality is far more important than quantity.

It will also be interesting to see how my list has changed this past year due to the various life events.

So stay tuned. This project isn’t done, not even on hold, but very much in progress.
